FAQ

Common Questions

Is Flavora free to use? +
Yes — the core kitchen experience and recipe generator are completely free. We offer a premium tier for advanced features like nutritional deep-dives, meal planning calendars, and community recipe sharing.
How accurate are the generated recipes? +
Our recipes are generated using a database of tested ingredient pairings, cooking methods, and flavor science. Every recipe includes realistic prep times, temperatures, and techniques that work in a real kitchen.
Can I save and share my creations? +
Absolutely. Every completed build can be saved to your personal recipe book, downloaded as a formatted card, or shared directly via link with friends and family.
What makes Flavora different from other recipe tools? +
Most recipe tools give you a search bar and a list. Flavora gives you a spatial kitchen where you explore ingredients visually, build meals interactively, and generate recipes instantly. It's designed to feel less like a tool and more like an experience.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes. On mobile devices, the 3D scenes are optimized for performance, and the builder uses touch-friendly panels and guided flows so you get the same great experience on any screen size.
